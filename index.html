About The Site

The purpose of this website is to give recomendations in film and popular music. However, it will try to avoid covering the most current or widely popular content. Instead, we will use our years of experience and enthusiasum to highlight some of the slightly more obscure films and music tracks out there. Anyone looking for the greatest film they've never heard of, or wanting to improve thier playlist with different, but still brilliant music, should find something on these lists.
