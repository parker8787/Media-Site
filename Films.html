About The Site

This page contains a list of films that I have found over the years and come to regard as my favourites. Now I can recomend them to everyone. Hopefully anyone reading this will be able to find a new favourite for themselves. Keep an eye out for them if they are ever shown on television. In my opinion they are all worth the price of a DVD too.
